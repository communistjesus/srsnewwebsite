How to Get Involved

We welcome new members who share our political orientation and aspirations!
We meet on Tuesday evenings from 7–9 pm PST on Zoom (bit.ly/srszoomlink) and in person at Christ Episcopal Church (4548 Brooklyn Ave NE, Seattle, WA 98105), unless otherwise stated. For confirmation, please see our calendar.
Typically, the first half of our general meeting consists of presentations and discussions on topics relevant to revolutionary socialism, while the second half is our business meeting in which we discuss local organizing efforts, engage in democratic decision-making, and hear reports from comrades, committees, and work groups. We welcome participation from anyone else interested in socialism or changing the world.
If you’re interested in joining our organization or just our mailing list, fill out the form below and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
